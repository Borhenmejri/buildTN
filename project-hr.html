About This Project
A comprehensive HR analytics report built to help HR managers and executives understand their workforce at a glance. The report covers 8 departments and 1,247 employees, analyzing turnover trends with root cause breakdown, recruitment funnel conversion rates, annual performance review distribution, salary equity analysis, training ROI, employee satisfaction scores, and a prioritized action plan with 6 data-backed recommendations.
